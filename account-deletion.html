Delete Your Account & Data

Expense Tracker · Last updated: July 8, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Expense Tracker account and all associated data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to expensetracker.enquires@gmail.com from the Google account you use to sign in (or state that email address in the message), with the subject Delete my account.

We will verify the request and delete your account and data, usually within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account identity (name, email, and account identifier)
  • All your transactions, categories, and household data

Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.

Data retention

We do not retain your personal data after deletion, except where required by law.
